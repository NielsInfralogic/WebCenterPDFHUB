Zoom view
The Zoom view is used to inspect a given page, selected in the thumbnail view. The preview file is loaded (typically 72dpi) and displayed on top of the thumbnail view.
Menu
The menu bar above the image zoom view is used to approve, disapprove and go back to the thumbnail view
Buttons
The buttons may be used for zooming.
Also the zoom can be controlled via click on the mouse left button.
Click to zoom in and click while holding down the shift key to zoom out.
