Thumbnail view
The Pages view shows a thumbnail gallery of all live pages in the system. The view can be filtered by clicking on a specific publication, edition or section in the tree view.
Menu
The menu bar above the thumbnails are used to refresh the display and to set the thumbnail size and auto-refresh time.
Thumbnails
Clicking on a thumbnail will open up the image zoom window for further preview inspection.
Background colors of the thumbnails represents the current approval state of the pages.
Green frame means approved. Red means rejected due to bad page contents and Gray means missing approval decision
